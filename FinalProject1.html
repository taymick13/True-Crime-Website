The genre that has taken the world by morbid curiosity!


True crime is a nonfiction literary, podcast, and film genre in which the author examines an actual crime and details the actions of real people associated with and affected by criminal events. The crimes most commonly include murder; about 40 percent focus on tales of serial killers. True crime comes in many forms, such as books, films, podcasts, and television shows. True crime works can impact the crimes they cover and the audience who consumes it.

Many individuals have consumed true crime podcasts, movies and books in extreme fashion over the past 10 years. Within the last 5 years, unsolved murders and crimes have become solved due to the attention brought to the cases. Individuals will attribute their love of true crime from Sarah Koenig's podcast show called Serial. We fell in love with the tantilizing information she gave us throughout the episodes. There are now a variety of podcasts, tv shows, movies and books about true crime that anyone can find a story that they find interesting and educational.

Below you will find a few of the most popular podcasts of 2022!
  • Morbid: A True Crime Podcast: Started by Alaina and Ash, two cousins, (who are more like sister) share stories from every century. They have witty banter between the two and are able to empathize with the victims while still addressing the seriousness of the crimes. (Wondery)
  • Crime Junkies: Stars Ashley and Brit tell the stories of individuals who have had the ultimate crimes committed against them and their families. They bring a real picture to the crimes and ask for the publics help to solve the crimes that remain open. Between Ashley, Brit and AudioChuck they have set up foundations to help solve the unsolveable and have been able to do this with the help of listeners. (AudioChuck)
  • Unsolved Murders: True Crime Stories: Unsolved murders and crimes told by an ensemble of cast members. They dive into the history and the inner workings of the crimes. Told in true storyteller fashion, the episodes bring to life the cold cases and unsolved murders. (Parcast)
